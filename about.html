About Hotel XO

Set within the popular and historic Baščaršija neighborhood, this hip hotel is 11 km from Sarajevo International Airport. It's 4 minutes by foot from Latin Bridge. Bright, colorful rooms feature modern furnishings, and offer free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and air-conditioning, plus minifridges and safes. Suites add separate bedrooms. Room service is available. A breakfast buffet is served in a restaurant's trendy dining room or on a terrace. Additional amenities include parking, a garden and massage services, plus an airport shuttle.
